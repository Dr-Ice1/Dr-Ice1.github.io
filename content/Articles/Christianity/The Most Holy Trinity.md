# Introduction

This is an article to highlight the Holy Trinity in the Bible, philosophy, and the Church Fathers.

# A Biblical case for Trinitarianism

In this part of the article a biblical case will be made for the Holy Trinity which will make two things clear. Firstly, that there is a multi-personality in God which the Scriptures communicate, specifically the Old Testament slowly uncovers these mysteries, but the New Testament fully reveals it through the Incarnation of Christ.

## Multi personality of God in OT

The beginning is of course what the Scriptures communicate as the beginning: "In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth." The Holy Trinity is already seen in the first three verses of the Bible, but in order to keep this article as short as possible, only the clear and undeniable cases will be highlighted.

From these verses some attributes can be deduced:
1. The Name of the Lord is in the Angel
2. Moses identifies the Angel as God
3. The Angel is called the God of Bethel referencing [Genesis 28:10-22](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Genesis%2028%3A10-22&version=NIV)
4. Fights and blesses Jacob, cf. Hosea 12:2-5
5. The Angel multiplies the descendants of Sarai
6. Commands Moses from within the bush
7. People are afraid to die after seeing him
